Dipped Ice Cream Sandwich

The finale is here!

Who doesn't love an ice cream sandwich? That would be good enough, right? Well you're going to step it up! A simple dip in chocolate and some sprinkles will add some extra goodness to this already perfect dessert. And we still don't need any silverware!

Ingredients

  • 6 semi-sweet chocolate baking squares, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon shortening
  • 4 ice cream sandwiches
  • colorful candy sprinkles

Instructions

  1. Line a baking sheet with waxed paper; set aside.
  2. In a microwave or heavy saucepan, melt the chocolate and shortening together and stir until smooth.
  3. Quickly dip the ice cream sandwiches halfway lengthwise in melted chocolate; coat chocolate with sprinkles.
  4. Place on prepared baking sheet and freeze.
